Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is your hair 100% human hair?
Yes! All b_lit hairs products are made from 100% virgin human hair — no synthetic blends, no shortcuts. Fully cuticle-aligned for maximum longevity and natural look.
Do you deliver across Ghana?
Absolutely. We deliver to all regions across Ghana including Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Tamale, Cape Coast and beyond. Chat with us on WhatsApp for delivery timelines and fees.
Can I colour or heat-style the hair?
Yes — because it's 100% virgin human hair, you can colour, bleach, flat iron, curl and style it just like your natural hair. We recommend professional colouring for best results.
How long does the hair last?
With proper care and maintenance, our hair can last 1–2 years (bundles) and 6–12 months for wigs used daily. We'll share our care guide with every order.
How do I place an order?
Simply click any product to view its details and tap the "Buy on WhatsApp" button. A pre-filled message will be sent to our team who will confirm your order, size, and delivery details instantly.
