Frequently Asked Questions

Is your hair 100% human hair?
Yes! All b_lit hairs products are made from 100% virgin human hair — no synthetic blends, no shortcuts. Fully cuticle-aligned for maximum longevity and natural look.
Do you deliver across Ghana?
Absolutely. We deliver to all regions across Ghana — Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Tamale, Cape Coast and beyond. Chat with us on WhatsApp for delivery timelines and fees.
Can I colour or heat-style the hair?
Yes — because it's 100% virgin human hair, you can colour, bleach, flat iron, curl and style it just like your natural hair. We recommend professional colouring for best results.
How do I place an order?
Click any product card, then tap "Buy on WhatsApp". A pre-filled message opens in WhatsApp — just send it and our team will confirm your order, size and delivery details immediately.
How long does the hair last?
With proper care, our bundles last 1–2 years and wigs last 6–12 months with daily use. We include a free care guide with every order.
