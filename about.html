The Founder

Meet Belinda

b_lit hairs was born from a personal frustration. Belinda, a passionate lover of beautiful hair, struggled for years to find high-quality, authentic virgin human hair in Ghana without overpaying or being deceived by synthetic blends.

In 2021, she decided to change that — sourcing directly from trusted suppliers abroad, building relationships based on integrity, and bringing world-class luxury hair directly to Ghanaian women at fair prices.

Today, b_lit hairs serves hundreds of women across Ghana, from Accra to Kumasi, Tamale to Takoradi — all united by one thing: their love of beautiful, authentic, long-lasting hair.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small idea to Ghana's most loved luxury hair brand — here's our story in milestones.

2021

The Beginning

Belinda places her first small bulk order, selling to friends and family out of her home in Accra. Word spreads quickly about the quality.

2022

Going Online

b_lit hairs launches on Instagram and WhatsApp. First 100 customers served. Nationwide delivery begins.

2023

Expanding the Range

New product lines added — wigs, frontals, and custom colour options. Partnerships with bridal stylists across Accra and Kumasi.

2024 →

The Future

Website launch, Supabase-powered product catalogue, and plans to serve even more queens across West Africa and beyond.
