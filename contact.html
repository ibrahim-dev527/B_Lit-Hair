Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast do you respond to messages?
On WhatsApp, we typically respond within minutes during business hours (Mon–Sat, 8AM–8PM). For emails, expect a reply within 24 hours. WhatsApp is always the fastest way to reach us.
Can I visit your shop in person?
Yes! We do in-person consultations and pickups in Accra. Please WhatsApp us first to schedule your visit so we can ensure someone is available to assist you properly.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?
Yes, we offer special wholesale pricing for bulk orders. If you're a stylist, salon owner, or reseller looking to stock b_lit hairs products, please WhatsApp us with "WHOLESALE" to get started.
What if I'm not happy with my order?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If there's an issue with your order — wrong item, damage, or quality concern — WhatsApp us within 48 hours of delivery with photos and we'll make it right immediately.
How do I track my delivery?
Once your order is dispatched, we'll send you a tracking update via WhatsApp with the rider's contact and estimated arrival time. For Accra deliveries, we often do real-time updates.
