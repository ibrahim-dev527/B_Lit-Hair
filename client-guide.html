How Your Website Works

Before we get into the details, here's a simple picture of how everything connects.

🌍

Your Website (5 pages)

Homepage, Shop, Services, About and Contact. Your customers see these. They automatically show whatever you add in the admin.

🔐

Admin Dashboard

This is your private control room at admin.html. Only you can log in. Everything you do here instantly updates your live website.

🗄

Database (Supabase)

This is where all your data is stored safely in the cloud — your products, reviews, services, messages. Think of it as your digital filing cabinet.

💡
The Simple Rule

You only ever use admin.html to manage your website. You never need to touch any other file. Add a product in admin → it appears in your shop. Approve a review → it shows on your homepage. Simple!

📱

What Your Customers See

They visit your website, browse products, read reviews, and either message you via the contact form or order directly through WhatsApp. You receive all of it.

💕

What You Control

Every single thing on the website — products, prices, photos, descriptions, services, reviews, and even hiding things temporarily. You are in full control.

3
How to Hide a Product (Without Deleting It)

Sometimes you might run out of stock and want to temporarily remove a product from the shop without permanently deleting it.

1
Find the product in your listLook for the eye 👁 icon in the Actions column.
2
Click the eye 👁 iconThe product disappears from your website immediately. The status badge changes to "Hidden" in red.
3
To bring it backClick the eye icon again. The product reappears on the website instantly. The status changes back to "Visible" in green.
💡
Use this for out-of-stock items

Instead of deleting a product when you run out of stock, just hide it. When your new stock arrives, click the eye icon again and it's back!

4
How to Delete a Product Permanently
1
Find the product in your listLook for the red trash 🗑 icon.
2
Click the trash iconA confirmation box appears asking "Are you sure?"
3
Click "Delete" to confirmThe product is permanently removed from the website and the database.
⚠️
This cannot be undone

Once you delete a product, it is gone forever. If you are unsure, use the Hide option instead — it removes it from the website but keeps it in your admin.

Chapter 5

Managing Customer Reviews

Reviews build trust and convince new customers to buy. Here is how to add and manage them.

⭐
Important: Reviews are hidden by default

Every review you add starts as "Pending" — it is NOT visible on the website until you approve it. This gives you full control over what customers see. Always remember to approve a review after adding it!

1
How to Add a Review from a Happy Customer

When a customer sends you a nice message on WhatsApp, you can add it to your website as a review.

1
Click "Reviews" in the sidebar, then "Add Review"
2
Fill in the reviewer's nameUse their first name and first letter of their last name for privacy — e.g. "Abena K."
3
Add their locatione.g. "Accra, Ghana" or "Kumasi, Ghana"
4
Select the star ratingChoose 1–5 stars. Most happy customers deserve 5 stars! ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
5
Type or paste the review textCopy exactly what they said on WhatsApp, or write a paraphrased version.
6
Tick the "Approved" checkboxThis makes it visible on the website immediately after saving. If you forget to tick this, the review will be saved but hidden.
7
Click "Save Review"It appears on the homepage and about page right away.
2
How to Approve or Hide a Review
1
Find the review in your listPending reviews show an orange "Pending" badge. Approved ones show a green "Approved" badge.
2
Click the green "Approve" buttonThe review immediately appears on your website for all customers to see.
3
To hide it againClick "Unapprove" — it goes back to hidden without being deleted.
💡
Aim for at least 6 reviews

Having 6+ reviews makes your website look established and trustworthy to new customers. Collect reviews from your WhatsApp customers and add them here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Things You Might Wonder About

I added a product but it's not showing on the shop. Why?
Check two things: First, make sure the "Visible on website" checkbox was ticked when you saved the product. Second, go to the product in admin, click the eye icon to check its status shows as "Visible" (green badge). If it shows "Hidden" (red badge), click the eye icon to make it visible again.
I approved a review but it's not on the homepage. Why?
Try refreshing the homepage (press Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R to force refresh). Also confirm the review shows a green "Approved" badge in the Reviews section of admin. If it shows orange "Pending", click the Approve button again.
How do I change the price of a product?
Go to Products, find the product, click the pencil ✏️ edit icon, change the price number, and click Save Product. The new price appears on the website within seconds.
Can I manage the website from my phone?
Yes! The admin dashboard works on phones too. Open your browser (Chrome, Safari), go to your admin link, and log in. Everything works on mobile — you can add products, upload photos from your gallery, approve reviews and read messages all from your phone.
A customer ordered via WhatsApp. Do I need to do anything in the admin?
No — WhatsApp orders are handled directly on WhatsApp, not through the admin. The "Buy on WhatsApp" button on the website sends the customer directly to your WhatsApp number with a pre-written message. You reply on WhatsApp and process the order there as usual.
What if I forget my admin password?
Open the admin.html file in a text editor (Notepad or Notes app). Search for ADMIN_PASSWORD and you will see the password there. You can change it to something new and re-upload the file to your hosting.
How many products can I have?
As many as you like! Supabase's free plan supports thousands of rows of data. You will never hit a limit for a hair business.
Do customers have to create an account to buy?
No — there are no customer accounts. Customers simply browse the shop, click "Buy on WhatsApp" on any product, and it opens WhatsApp with a ready-made message. The sale is completed on WhatsApp where you confirm details, receive payment (MoMo) and arrange delivery. Simple and fast!
How do I make a product appear on the homepage?
Add a Badge to the product — such as "Best Seller", "New Arrival", or "Hot Pick". Products with any badge text automatically appear in the "Fan Favourites" section on the homepage. Products without a badge only appear on the Shop page.
